I do all kinds of things, but still find time to do nothing.

I have been a meatcutter, baker, line cook, chocolate maker, and sustainable agriculture advocate, and I am currently learning to program at Dev Boot Camp.

I am from Vermont, live in Chicago, and have lived in Australia and Boston. I have a rabbit named Pat the Bunny, named after the children's book and the SNL skit "It's Pat." I do lots of yoga at CorePower Yoga. I am currently growing portobella mushrooms in my house.

I am strangely entranced by real estate and Detroit, despite never having been there.

I love meat, and I have been working on a readable guide to meat for the past seven years. I believe an app is in order.
